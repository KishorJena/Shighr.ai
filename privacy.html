Privacy Policy

Your Privacy, Our Priority

Hey there! I’m Kishor Jena, the creator of Shighr.ai, a Chrome extension designed to give you fast AI chat answers right in your browser. I’ve built this to be simple and respect your privacy—here’s how it works.

What We Collect (Spoiler: Not Much!)

In the current version of Shighr.ai:

  • No Server Storage: We don’t send your data to any servers or cloud. Everything stays on your device.
  • Local Storage: Your prompts and settings are stored locally using Chrome’s chrome.storage.local. This keeps things fast and private, only on your computer.
  • Your API Key: You provide your own Groq API key (grabbed from Groq by you). We don’t store it anywhere—Shighr.ai just uses it to send your prompts to Groq and get responses back.

How It Works

Shighr.ai overlays an AI assistant on your active tab using a Shadow DOM. This means it doesn’t touch or mess with the webpage you’re on—just floats above it, safe and separate.

Third Parties

We rely on Groq to process your prompts and generate answers. That’s it! Your prompt goes straight to Groq using your API key, and their response comes back to you. Check Groq’s Privacy Policy for how they handle things on their end.

Future Updates

This policy covers the current version of Shighr.ai. If we add features in the future that involve storing or processing data differently (like on a server), we’ll update this page first and let you know. No surprises!

Questions?

I’m not here to hide behind fine print. If you’ve got questions, ideas, or concerns about privacy, reach out! Find me on GitHub, LinkedIn, or X.
